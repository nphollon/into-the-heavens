Version 7 - June 17, 2016

  • Player ship moves at a fixed speed.
  • Player ship turns much slower.
  • Adjust "Flight Practice" to make it easier to avoid crashing into the planet.

Version 6 - June 6, 2016

  • Upgrade to Elm 0.17.

Version 5 - May 31, 2016

  • Previous game version converted to a level called "Outnumbered."
  • "Outnumbered" has bigger waves of enemies.
  • "Outnumbered" has a win condition.
  • Three new levels: "Flight Practice," "One By One," and "Pavilion."
  • Level selection menu.
  • Events can be triggered by checkpoints.
  • Better collision detection (using OBBTree algorithm).
  • More detailed model for enemy ships.
  • Planets are solid-colored.

Version 4 - March 22, 2016

  • Enemy ships fly and fire towards player.
  • Player has a shield that blocks missiles.
  • Shield consumes energy. Energy recharges when shield is off.
  • Multiple enemies appear at once.
  • Player can switch targets.

Version 3 - March 4, 2016

  • Enemy ship fires seeker missiles at random.
  • Enemy ship is circled in blue. Enemy missiles are circled in red
  • Background stars form a globular cluster.

Version 2 - February 24, 2016

  • Player's missiles seek out visiting ships.
  • Missiles self-destruct after three seconds.
  • Add reticule to center of vision.
